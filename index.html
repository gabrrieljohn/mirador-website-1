Your Questions, Answered

We're a true multi-specialist practice. Unlike most clinics where one dentist handles everything, our clinic has a dedicated orthodontist, prosthodontist, endodontist, paediatric dentist, oral surgeon, and general dentists — each working only within their area of expertise. It's the hospital model applied to dentistry.

Dr. Joseph is an American-trained, American Board-Certified orthodontist with over 30 years of experience in Cochin. He holds an M.S. from Ohio State University and was Chairman of the Indian Board of Orthodontics. He has lectured at international conferences including the American Association of Orthodontists. His practice is limited exclusively to orthodontics.

Yes. Dr. Anila Valsan, our paediatric dentist with an MDS in Pedodontics, specialises exclusively in children's dental health — from preventive care and habit correction to managing dental injuries. Dr. Rekha Thomas also has a special interest in preventive dental care for children.

With modern techniques and our specialist endodontist, root canal treatment is typically comfortable and completed in 1 to 3 visits. A crown is placed afterward to protect and strengthen the tooth. The treated tooth can last a lifetime with proper care.

Our prosthodontist offers dental implants, fixed bridges, removable partial or complete dentures, crowns, and porcelain veneers. The best option depends on the number and location of missing teeth, the condition of remaining teeth, and your preferences. We'll discuss all options with you.

Yes — credit card and debit card payment facilities are available at the clinic.
